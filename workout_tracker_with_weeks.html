Workout Tracker

Week 1

Week 1: Full Body Strength & Compound Lifts

  • Squats (4 sets x 8-10 reps)
  • Deadlifts (3 sets x 5-8 reps)
  • Bench Press (4 sets x 8-10 reps)
  • Bicep Curls (3 sets x 10-12 reps)

  • Bicep Curls (3 sets x 10-12 reps)
  • Triceps Pushdowns (3 sets x 10-12 reps)
  • Leg Raises (3 sets x 12-15 reps)

Week 3: Pushing Movements + Arms

  • Shoulder Press (4 sets x 8-10 reps)
  • Squats (3 sets x 8-10 reps)
  • Bench Press (3 sets x 8-10 reps)
  • Tricep Dips (3 sets x 8-10 reps)
  • Russian Twists (3 sets x 15-20 reps)
